Satellite photographed a new lava flow on the island of Palma

The European Space Agency has published fresh images of a new lava flow on the island of Palma. Fresh pictures show exactly how the lava flows - now there are two streams, they run parallel to each other.

The rocket "Unioun-2.1b" installed on the start table

The "Union-2.1b" rocket, whose fifth launch this year is scheduled for October 14, was installed in the launch system at the Vostochny cosmodrome. It is planned to deliver British OneWeb communications satellites into orbit

Rocket tests stopped in Voronezh to save oxygen

ROSCOSMOS stopped testing rocket engines in Voronezh by the end of October. The saved oxygen will be transferred to medical institutions.
