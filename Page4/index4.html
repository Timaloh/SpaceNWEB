Famous Images

"Greys" are humanoid creatures with a flabby body covered with gray or light green skin, without hair and with a disproportionately large head, on which there are huge slanted eyes of black color and almond shape;

"Nords" are the aliens most similar to humans, namely the Caucasians of the Northern type, tall with geometrically perfect facial features and physique;

What is Aliens?

A humanoid alien, as a rule, is distinguished by erectness, pronounced lower and upper limbs, paired external signs oriented along a vertical axis. Thus, the classic image of the alien-gray is characterized by the non-obvious absence of sex signs in him as a biological species: there are no pronounced external genitalia and sexual dimorphism. Most descriptions of extraterrestrials' appearance and behavior present them as neuter, asexual, and impersonal beings.

"Nords", on the contrary, are characterized by pronounced and inherent sexual dimorphism.

In the vast majority of cases, in the representation of terrestrial people, aliens are representatives of civilizations that are significantly superior to earthlings technologically (since the latter have mastered interstellar movements). According to these assumptions, extraterrestrials, as a rule, come to Earth on spaceships (UFOs), sometimes kidnap people (grays) or, without any compulsion, invite them to board and make a trip ("nords"), and also communicate with contactees.
